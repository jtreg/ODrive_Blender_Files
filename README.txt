# Summary

Mounts a Tower Pro 9 gram micro servo.
And a Fitec 9 gram continuous micro servo. 
Use two sheet-metal Phillips oval 4 x 3/4 screws to secure the servo to the mount (see picture above). 
The mount gets secured to a base by three  M3 * 5 mounting screws.




# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Printrbot
Printer: Simple Black
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: fine
Infill: 15%

Notes: 
how I printed it, the base it was breaking a bit, so I recommend a higher Infill to prevent breaking.

# Instructions

1) feed the 3 pin female connector through the rectangular hole from the inside.
2) Push the servo in by the front.
3) screw the servo in place by two long thin screws.
4) secure the base in place by three M3 * 5 mounting screws.
